\begin{enumerate}
\item Find the solution of the IVP in the Solow’s growth model, $$\dot k=A\sqrt{k}-(n+\delta)k, \; k(0)=k_0,$$

where $A,n,\delta>0$.

\item Find the values $a$ and $b$ such that the function $f$ is homogeneous:
$$f(x,y)=2x^{b-a}y^{b+2}+y^{a+1}x^{-3b}+y^{7b}x^{-2a}.$$
For the values $a$ and $b$ you have found expand the function $$h(x)=\sqrt{1+f(x,x)}(1-\cos(f(x,x))$$
as a power series up to $x^4$. State the range for $x$ where your expansion is correct.

\item Maximize $3xy-x^3$ subject to the constraints
$$\begin{array}{l}
2x-y=-5,\\
5x+2y\geqslant 37,\\
x\geqslant 0,\; y\geqslant 0.
\end{array}
$$
\item Solve the following first-order differential equation $$(y^2-2xy)dx+x^2dy=0.$$

\item For what values of $(x_0,y_0)$ does the differential equation $xy'=\sqrt{x-y}$ have a unique solution $y=y(x)$ such that $y(x_0)=y_0$?
\end{enumerate}
